What is TheSubnetGuy?

TheSubnetGuy is a personal blog built to document, share, and simplify the world of Networking and Automation. It’s where theory meets practice — through real labs, configurations, and automation scripts.

My goal is to make complex network topics approachable for everyone: from CCNA students to seasoned network engineers curious about automation. This is more than a blog — it’s a growing archive of everything I’ve learned while building smarter networks.

🌐 Networking

Routing, switching, VLANs, and network design fundamentals.

⚙️ Automation

Python, Netmiko, Ansible, and scripting network configurations at scale.

🧠 Engineering

System design, reliability, and optimizing performance in real networks.
